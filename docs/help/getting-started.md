# Getting Started with Sparlo

Welcome to Sparlo, your engineering intelligence platform. This guide will help you get started quickly.

## What is Sparlo?

Sparlo provides AI-powered analysis and research reports for engineering and technology topics. Create comprehensive reports to understand technical concepts, analyze companies, or research market trends.

## Creating Your First Report

1. Click **New Analysis** in the sidebar
2. Choose your report type:
   - **Discovery Report**: Quick overview analysis for initial research
   - **Deep Dive Report**: Comprehensive analysis with detailed insights
3. Enter your topic or company name
4. Click **Generate** and wait for your report

## Understanding Report Types

### Discovery Reports
Quick 2-3 minute analyses perfect for:
- Initial research on a topic
- Quick company overviews
- Exploring new areas

### Deep Dive Reports
Comprehensive 5-10 minute analyses including:
- Detailed technical breakdowns
- Market analysis
- Competitive landscape
- Strategic recommendations

## Navigating Your Workspace

- **Dashboard**: View all your reports and activity
- **Reports**: Access your report library
- **Team**: Manage team members and permissions (team accounts only)
- **Settings**: Configure your account and preferences

## Tips for Better Reports

- Be specific with your topics for more focused analysis
- Use company names or product names for targeted research
- Save reports you want to reference later
- Share reports with your team for collaboration
