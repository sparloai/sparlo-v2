# Working with Reports

Learn how to create, view, share, and manage your Sparlo reports.

## Creating Reports

### Starting a New Report

1. Click the **New Analysis** button in the sidebar
2. Select your report type (Discovery or Deep Dive)
3. Enter your topic, company name, or research question
4. Click **Generate** to start the analysis

### Report Generation

Reports typically take 2-10 minutes depending on complexity. You can:
- Leave the page and return later
- Continue working while the report generates
- View progress in real-time

## Viewing Reports

### Report Structure

Reports include:
- **Executive Summary**: Key findings at a glance
- **Analysis Sections**: Detailed breakdowns by topic
- **Recommendations**: Actionable insights
- **Sources**: References and citations

### Navigation

- Use the table of contents to jump between sections
- Expand/collapse sections for easier reading
- Use the chat feature to ask follow-up questions

## Sharing Reports

### Share Link

1. Open the report you want to share
2. Click the **Share** button in the top right
3. Copy the share link
4. Send the link to anyone you want to share with

Shared links are read-only and do not require a Sparlo account.

### Export as PDF

1. Open the report
2. Click the **Export PDF** button
3. Wait for the PDF to generate
4. Download the file

## Managing Reports

### Archive Reports

To archive a report you no longer need:
1. Find the report in your list
2. Click the menu (three dots)
3. Select **Archive**

Archived reports can be restored anytime.

### Delete Reports

To permanently delete a report:
1. Archive the report first
2. Go to your archived reports
3. Click **Delete permanently**

Note: Deleted reports cannot be recovered.
