# Account and Billing

Manage your Sparlo account, subscription, and team settings.

## Account Types

### Personal Account

Your personal workspace for individual use:
- Private reports visible only to you
- Personal settings and preferences
- Individual billing

### Team Account

Shared workspace for organizations:
- Collaborate with team members
- Share reports within the team
- Centralized billing
- Role-based permissions

## Subscription Plans

### Free Plan
- Limited reports per month
- Basic analysis features
- Personal use only

### Pro Plan
- Unlimited reports
- Deep Dive analysis
- Priority processing
- PDF exports
- Chat with reports

### Team Plan
- Everything in Pro
- Team collaboration
- Admin controls
- Priority support

## Managing Your Subscription

### Upgrade Your Plan

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **Upgrade**
3. Select your new plan
4. Complete payment

Your upgrade takes effect immediately.

### Cancel Subscription

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **Cancel subscription**
3. Confirm cancellation

You keep access until the end of your billing period.

## Team Management

### Invite Team Members

1. Go to your team workspace
2. Click **Members** in the sidebar
3. Click **Invite member**
4. Enter their email address
5. Select their role

### Team Roles

- **Owner**: Full access, can delete team
- **Admin**: Manage members and settings
- **Member**: Create and view reports

### Remove Team Members

1. Go to **Members**
2. Find the member
3. Click the menu (three dots)
4. Select **Remove from team**

## Payment Methods

### Update Payment Method

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **Update payment method**
3. Enter new card details
4. Save changes

### View Invoices

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **Billing history**
3. Download any invoice as PDF
